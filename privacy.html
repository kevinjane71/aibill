Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

Welcome to Zoby. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Zoby is designed to keep your data private and secure. We collect minimal information:

  • Subscription Data: Information you manually enter about your subscriptions (names, amounts, renewal dates, etc.)
  • Device Permissions: Notification permissions to send renewal reminders

2. How We Store Your Data

Local Storage Only:

  • All subscription data is stored locally on your device
  • We do NOT collect, transmit, or store your subscription information on our servers
  • Your financial data never leaves your device
  • We do not have access to your data

3. Notifications

  • We use iOS notification permissions to send renewal reminders
  • These notifications are generated locally on your device
  • No notification data is sent to external servers
  • You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal identification information (name, email,phone number)
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • We do NOT use third-party analytics or tracking services
  • We do NOT display advertisements
  • We do NOT sell or share your data with anyone

5. In-App Purchases

If you purchase Zoby Premium:

  • Your payment is processed securely through Apple's App Store
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • Apple handles all payment processing and receipts

6. Data Security

  • Your data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, biometric authentication, etc.)
  • All data is stored using industry-standard secure storage methods
  • Only you have access to your subscription data

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data remains on your device until you delete the app
  • You can delete individual subscriptions at any time within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all data from your device

8. Children's Privacy

Zoby is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Modify: Edit or delete any subscription at any time
  • Delete: Remove all data by uninstalling the app

11. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The only third-party service we use is Apple's App Store for in-app purchases.
