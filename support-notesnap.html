‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I capture a whiteboard or note?
Tap the Camera button on the home screen. Point your camera at the whiteboard or note, and tap to capture. Our AI will automatically extract any action items or tasks from the image.
What is Brain Dump mode?
Brain Dump is a voice recording feature that lets you speak your thoughts freely. Simply tap the microphone button and start talking. NoteSnap will transcribe your voice and automatically extract action items from what you said.
How does Focus Mode work?
Focus Mode is a Pomodoro-style timer that helps you concentrate on one task at a time. Choose your duration (5, 15, 25, or 45 minutes), and the app will guide you through focused work sessions with breaks in between.
How do I mark a task as complete?
On the Actions screen, you can mark a task complete by:
  • Tapping the circular checkbox on the left
  • Tapping the green checkmark button on the right
Can I use the app offline?
Yes! NoteSnap works offline. Your captures and tasks are saved locally and will sync to the cloud when you're back online.
How do I delete a task?
On the Actions screen, tap the X (close) button on any task card. You'll be asked to confirm before the task is permanently removed.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel:
  • Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  • Tap your name at the top
  • Tap Subscriptions
  • Find NoteSnap and tap Cancel Subscription
Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Open NoteSnap, go to the subscription screen, and tap "Restore" in the top right corner. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.
