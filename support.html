Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a subscription?

1. Tap the "+" button on the home screen
2. Enter your subscription details (name, amount, billing cycle, next renewal date)
3. Optionally add a category and notes
4. Tap "Save"

How do renewal reminders work?

AIBill sends you smart notifications before your subscriptions renew. By default, you'll receive a reminder 3 days before renewal. You can customize this in Settings or set custom reminders for individual subscriptions.

Example notification:
"ChatGPT Plus renews in 3 days for $20.00"

Is my data safe?

Absolutely! All your subscription data is stored locally on your device. We never send your data to our servers or share it with anyone. Your financial information stays 100% private.

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

Free: Track up to 5 subscriptions with basic features
Premium: Unlimited subscriptions, advanced analytics, export data, and priority support

How do I restore my Premium purchase?

1. Open the app and go to Settings
2. Tap "Upgrade to Premium"
3. Tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom
4. Your Premium access will be restored if you've previously purchased it with this Apple ID

How do I edit or delete a subscription?

To Edit: Tap on any subscription card to view details, then tap "Edit"
To Delete: View subscription details and tap "Delete Subscription" at the bottom

Can I export my subscription data?

Yes! Premium users can export their subscription data. This feature is coming in a future update.

I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?

1. Check that notifications are enabled in Settings → Notifications → Renewal Reminders
2. Make sure AIBill has notification permissions in your iPhone Settings → AIBill → Notifications
3. Verify your subscriptions have renewal dates set
4. Ensure your renewal date is in the future

How do I cancel a subscription?

AIBill is a tracking app - it doesn't manage your actual subscriptions. To cancel a subscription, you need to contact the service provider directly or cancel through your Apple ID subscriptions if it's an App Store subscription.

Once canceled, you can delete it from AIBill or mark it as inactive.

What billing cycles are supported?

AIBill supports:
• Monthly
• Yearly
• Weekly
• Custom (for any other billing period)

Can I track free trials?

Yes! When adding a subscription, you can mark it as a trial and set the trial end date. AIBill will remind you before the trial ends so you can decide whether to continue or cancel.

Troubleshooting

App is crashing or freezing

1. Force close the app and restart it
2. Restart your iPhone
3. Check for app updates in the App Store
4. If the issue persists, contact support at support@aibill.app

My subscriptions aren't showing up

Make sure you've added subscriptions by tapping the "+" button. Your data is stored locally, so subscriptions won't appear until you manually add them.

Premium purchase isn't working

1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify your Apple ID payment method is valid
3. Try "Restore Purchases" if you've already purchased Premium
4. Contact us at support@aibill.app if the issue continues
